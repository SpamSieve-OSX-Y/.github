## 🖌 Overview  
SpamSieve Advanced is a highly accurate spam filtering application that integrates directly with your email client to block unwanted messages before they reach your inbox. Using a Bayesian learning engine, it adapts to your personal mail patterns and improves its accuracy over time.

Setup is straightforward. Connect it to Apple Mail or another supported client, let it learn from a sample of your good and spam messages, and it immediately begins filtering with accuracy that beats any built-in solution.

The whitelist and blocklist systems give you manual control alongside the automatic filtering. Trusted senders always get through; known spammers are always blocked regardless of how their messages are phrased.

Detailed statistics show you exactly how much spam has been caught, how accurate the filtering is, and which rules are contributing most to the decisions being made.

For anyone overwhelmed by unwanted email, SpamSieve Advanced is the filtering layer that restores calm to the inbox without ever losing a legitimate message.
